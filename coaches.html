Meet Our Instructors:

Joao Oliveira

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

Training full time since 2010 Joao has earned a brown belt in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. He has competed in Kickboxing , BJJ, Wrestling and MMA both at local and International level winning National and International titles along the way. Completing a Diploma in Health Fitness and Wellbeing and a Bachelor of Arts in Sport Coaching and Business Management while receiving a scholarship for Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, one of two people in the country to do so to date.

Roisin Murphy

Kickboxing

Roisin has almost 9 years of martial arts training in multiple martial arts including Judo, Kickboxing, and MMA. She has a Bachelor Degree in Physical Education awarded by UCD. She has been training at Anaconda for 6 years and has been coaching kids and adults Kickboxing for almost 3 years. She has competed in many tournaments across the world and she is an WKF certified Referee and Refereed at multiple Kickboxing events across the country.


About Kickboxing…

Arslan Johnston

Boxing

Arslan has over 10 years Boxing experience in both competition and coaching. He is an active competitor and has multiple National and International medals. Most notable being his 2018 European Gold Medal that he won in Lisbon. As one of co-founders of Anaconda academy Arslan has been instrumental in creating the school’s Boxing curriculum and he has coached many National and European level Contenders and two National level Champions.
